CS247 HCI Design Studio

Final Presentation

Friday, March 11, 6pm - 8pm

Stanford d.school (550 Panama Mall, Stanford)

Come and check out how 80 Stanford students solve real-world problems with programming and design.

There will be 24 teams presenting their projects.

Agenda

  • 5:30 - 6:00pm Social Mixer
  • Students set up and guests arrive.
  • 6:00pm - 6:15pm Introduction
  • 6:15pm - 7:00pm Elevator Pitches
  • Each team presents a 30-second pitch of their work.
  • 7:00pm - 7:50pm Poster and Demo Session
  • Students present demos of their prototypes. Food will be served.
  • 7:50pm - 8:00pm Closing Remarks

Project themes:

  • Autonomous Things
  • Destinations
  • Leveling the Playing Field
  • Towards Invisible Interfaces
  • Home is Where the Heart Is
  • Technology for Human Being

Where is the d.school?

The d.school (Hasso Platner Institute of Design) lives in Building 550, in the center of campus.

Thomas F. Peterson Engineering Laboratory/Building 550
550 Panama Mall
Stanford, CA 94305

Parking

If you are visiting from off-campus, you should park at Tressider Visitor Parking and then walk to Building 550. Parking is free, starting at 4PM.

For more information, please visit http://dschool.stanford.edu/about/#getting-to-our-building
