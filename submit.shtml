Submission Procedure

To submit an assignment, go to canvas.stanford.edu. From there, go to CS247 (either from clicking on CS247 from the Dashboard or by clicking on Courses). Then click on Assignments -> P1 (for example) (left menu) -> Submit Assignment (top right).

After uploading your file, click Submit Assignment.

For group submissions, only one member needs to submit the deliverables for the whole group.
