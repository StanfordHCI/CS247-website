Interaction Design Studio

CS247 · Winter 2015

In this course, you will learn how to design technologies that bring people joy, rather than frustration. To do this, you'll learn techniques for rapidly brainstorming, prototyping and evaluating multiple interface alternatives — and why rapid prototyping and evaluation are essential to excellent interaction design. You'll learn to communicate your ideas visually. You'll deepen your experiences with needfinding and continuous feedback. And you will do all this in a design studio environment, learning to critique and be critiqued with industry leaders who have years of experience.
