1 | MOODBOARDS

Create a moodboard to reflect your creative vision. Find inspiration from Behance, Dribbble, Awwwards, Niice, Pinterest, Google, etc. and collect it in a Keynote, Powerpoint, Sketch, or other doc.

2 | GRIDS AND LAYOUT

Brainstorm and sketch three different layout ideas for your redesign. As you do this, focus on the different kinds of information and their heirarchy.

Start by drawing a screen no bigger than 5", and adding light grid columns. Then, start adding different elements in darker ink: a logo, navigation bar, pictures, text, or whatever else you think should go on the page.
