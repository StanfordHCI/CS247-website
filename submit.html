Submission Procedure

We will be using Dropbox for project submissions. You will receive a Dropbox folder invitation to your studio Dropbox folder from your TA. In the studio folder there will be a subfolder with your sunetid. This will be your submission folder for the rest of the quarter. For each project, create a folder inside your sunetid folder following the naming format "P#" (e.g. a folder named "P1" for "Project 1, On the Town: visual communication" that is in the path: /your_studio_folder/your_sunetid/P1/). Upload all of the project deliverables into this folder.

Frequently asked questions

Where do I find my CS247 Dropbox folder?

The TA for your studio will invite you to a shared Dropbox studio folder. The folder should contain a subfolder with your sunetid. Submit assignments within your sunetid folder. Expect an email with a Dropbox link to be sent to your Stanford address by the end of the first week, Sunday 01/11. If you haven't received an email by then, post a private Piazza question so that the TA can email you a new invitation.

What if I don't have a Dropbox account?

If you do not already have a Dropbox account under your Stanford email, please create one. If you have a personal reason for not using Dropbox, notify us by posting a private question on Piazza
